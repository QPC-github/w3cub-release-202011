Description

GtkGestureDrag is a GtkGesture implementation that recognizes drag operations. The drag operation itself can be tracked throughout the “drag-begin”, “drag-update” and “drag-end” signals, or the relevant coordinates be extracted through gtk_gesture_drag_get_offset() and gtk_gesture_drag_get_start_point().
